TRM for Children
This is a course for children aged between 9-13 years.

Children need to develop a concept of life skills to go hand in hand with academic knowledge. This course is designed to help children understand who they are and why they and others, think and act the way they do.



TRM for Teens
This is a course for teenagers

The teenage years can be a difficult time to navigate through both for the teens and their parents. At PDI we believe the narrative can be changed through life skills. This program is tailored for the teen who is answering questions about their identity and future.

TRM for Young Adults
This is a course for young adults eg:campus students

This program is tailored to transform the youth to think differently and to position them for the global changes inthe job marketand life in general. We believe that our youth can thrive while they are more self-aware.





TRM for Adults
A course unit for adults

The adults have the advantage of reflecting on their past experiences. This helps them to unlearn, learn and relearn. We havemany transformational stories from the adults that have gone through this class. The most celebrated modules in TRM adults are Life Purpose, Financial, Spiritual & Emotional Intelligence; Health and Wellness, writing of the 5 year strategic plan.


TRM for Entrepreneurs
A course unit for businesspeople and entrepreneurs

This is tailored for those transitioning from formal employment and for those repositioning ongoing businesses.The dynamics in the business world require constant learning and the ability to adapt to change. The modules taught in this course can be summed up as Thinking for a Change. This is purely one-to-one coaching that is client-driven based on the business needs.


TRM for the Retired to Refired
A unit for retired personel

This program is tailored to redefine the next phase of life after retirement. A meaningful and power-packed adventure is possible after retirement. At PDI we believe in being refired not retired. We have many success stories of those who came as retirees and refired their lives. You can live your best life fulfilling life goals and impacting generations through God-given purpose.
